# 📊 Amazon Pricing & Rating Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

![Dashboard Preview](Tableu_Dashboard1.jpeg)

🚀 Analyzed **1,465 Amazon products** to uncover pricing trends, discount strategies, and customer rating behavior.

🔑 **Key Insight:** Higher pricing and discounts do not necessarily lead to better ratings, revealing inefficiencies in pricing strategies.

---

## 📌 Project Summary

- Built an interactive **Tableau dashboard** to analyze product pricing and customer ratings  
- Performed data cleaning and transformation using **Excel and SQL**  
- Developed KPIs including:
  - Average Rating: **4.1**
  - Price Distribution  
  - Category-wise Performance  
- Implemented filters and parameters for dynamic, real-time analysis  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

### 🔹 Pricing & Rating Overview
- Top 10 Highest Rated Products  
- Rating Distribution (Binning)  
- Average Discount % by Category  
- Top Discounted Products  
- Product Count by Category  

### 🔹 Relationship Analysis
- Discount % vs Rating (Scatter Plot)  
- Review Count vs Rating (100+ reviews filter)

## 🔎 Key Insights

- Most products fall in the **4.0–4.5 rating range**, showing strong customer satisfaction  
- Discounts do **not negatively impact ratings**  
- Products with high review counts maintain ratings above 4.0 → strong trust factor  
- Electronics & Computers dominate product categories  
- Some products show **₹3000+ discounts**, indicating aggressive pricing strategies
